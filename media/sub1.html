About

BATTLE ROYALE

The Ring is always closing and you won't last long on the outside. As the Ring gets smaller, you'll be forced into more encounters with the remaining squads. So make sure you have the right loadout to take them on.

You'll drop in with basic gear. Explore the map -and Death Boxes of fallen foes- to find better equipment. If you're going to survive, you'll need it.

Whether you're in Duos or Trios, the goal is the same: to be the last squad left alive. But death isn't always permanent --keep your squad in the fight with the help of Respawn Beacons.

MAPS

  • BROKEN MOON
    Boreas’ moon, Cleo, was once whole-until a meteor and a myth broke it apart. It wasn’t until researchers discovered the link between the moon and an increase in devastating weather events that the nations of Boreas came together and promised to heal Cleo, naming their efforts Project Hope. However, long years and slow progress caused the peoples’ hearts and wallets to turn from Hope. This forced the Cleo Restoration Committee to privatize the moon for funding-with townhomes, shopping centers, and prestige burial plots added.
  • OLYMPUS
    Once a city built on dreams, Olympus was a utopia floating in clouds above Psamathe. However, an accident in an experimental research facility led to the creation of the Phase Rift, and the Outlands’ best and brightest quickly abandoned the city.
  • WORLD’S EDGE
    On Talos, a harsh planet surrounded by intense volcanic activity There is a World's Edge town. Then, an explosion occurred in one of the towers, and the surrounding area was covered in a deadly moment of freezing.
